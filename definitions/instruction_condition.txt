A medication instruction condition indicates the specific circumstances for the medication instruction, e.g. when and why to use the medication as instructed.
The text associated with the medication instruction condition can be in the same sentence or in a different sentence than the medication or the medication instruction or it can be a section heading.
Span the most specific text that signals the medication instruction condition.
The medication instruction condition can be expressed in an If-clause or statements like “Current Medications”.
If there is text indicating some instructions but no specific medication mentioned, ignore that instruction text.
